Programs We Offer

Computer Systems Servicing NC II (CSS NC II)

Master computer hardware, network setup, troubleshooting, and system maintenance with hands-on training.

  • Duration: 35 Days
  • Mode: Blended (Online, Modular, Face-to-Face)
  • Skills: PC Assembly, Network Configuration, Hardware Troubleshooting

Electronic Products Assembly & Servicing NC II (EPAS NC II)

Learn professional electronic assembly, soldering techniques, testing, and repair of electronic products.

  • Duration: 33 Days
  • Mode: Blended (Online, Modular, Face-to-Face)
  • Skills: PCB Assembly, Component Testing, Circuit Repair

Bread & Pastry Production NC II (BPP NC II)

Master the art of baking with comprehensive training in breads, cakes, pastries, and dessert preparation.

  • Duration: 18 Days
  • Mode: Blended (Online, Modular, Face-to-Face)
  • Skills: Bread Making, Cake Decoration, Pastry Techniques

Housekeeping NC II (HSK NC II)

Professional housekeeping training covering hotel standards, cleaning procedures, and hospitality excellence.

  • Duration: 55 Days
  • Mode: Blended (Online, Modular, Face-to-Face)
  • Skills: Room Cleaning, Laundry Operations, Guest Relations

Enrollment Process

Steps to Enroll

  1. Visit our office during business hours for consultation
  2. Submit required documents: 2x2, 1x1, Passport size picture (Chemically print), educational background Documents (Diploma, TOR, Form 137)
  3. Complete enrollment form with accurate personal and contact information
  4. Pay enrollment fee to secure your slot in the program
  5. Receive confirmation and training schedule details

Assessment Requirements

  • Completed Assessment Application Form
  • passport-size ID pictures (recent)
  • Photocopy of valid government-issued ID
  • Supporting documents (educational certificates, work experience)
  • Assessment fee payment

Certification & Recognition

Certificate of Completion

Official certificate awarded upon successfully completing your chosen training program, recognized by employers nationwide.

  • Issued immediately after program completion
  • Includes competencies achieved and training hours
  • Valid for TESDA NC assessment application

TESDA National Certificate (NC)

Government-issued certificate awarded by TESDA after passing the competency assessment, your gateway to better employment opportunities.

  • Nationally and internationally recognized
  • Lifetime validity for qualified professionals
  • Required for many technical jobs and overseas employment
